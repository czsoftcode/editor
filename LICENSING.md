PolyCredo Editor follows a dual-licensing model to ensure it remains open-source for the community while providing professional options for commercial users.

- You are free to use, modify, and distribute the software.
- If you modify the software and run it as a service or distribute it, you must provide the source code of your modifications under the same AGPLv3 license.
- This ensures that all improvements to the community version remain available to everyone.

See the [LICENSE](LICENSE) file for the full text of the AGPLv3.

## Commercial Edition & Corporate Use

For companies and organizations that require a different set of terms or wish to support the project commercially, we offer:

1. **Commercial Licenses**: Tailored for enterprise environments where AGPLv3 compliance may not be suitable.
2. **Sponsorship**: Active sponsors have a direct influence on the project's roadmap through voting and priority support.
3. **Certified Builds**: Pre-compiled, signed, and officially supported binaries for production use.

If you are interested in a commercial license or custom build, please contact the project owners.
